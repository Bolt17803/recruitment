RESPONSIBILITIES OF CORE-MEMBER
  • Core members should attend club meetings, workshops, and events regularly. They are expected to actively engage in club activities and contribute to discussions and planning.

  • Core members often assist in organizing and coordinating events, workshops, and photo outings. They may also help with administrative tasks, such as handling social media.

  • We are also looking for members who have experience in designing, handling social media and editing.  If you are interested to be a part of the club(if not already) and are proficient in any of the above,  then we encourage you to apply.
  • Core members will also get a chance to be a part of creative photoshoot  such as themed based photo series and get opportunities to cover various college events.
 

Perks of becoming Core member
  • Core members will typically handle the camera frequently and will definitely see a rise in their photography skills overtime.
  • High chance of contributing in inter-IIT Cultural Meet 6.0
  • A chance to enhance management and organizational skills which will be beneficial in future roles.
  • Core members have a means to promote their vision and ideas within the club, 
    helping shape the direction and focus of club activities.
  • At the end of the tenure, each core member will have a Position of Responsibility certificate.
 

What we do ?
  • We conduct various workshops to teach different photography techniques to our members.
  • We also teach the handling of DSLR camera to make our members familiar with DSLRs.
  • We give regular tasks to our members so as to get a hands on practice in various techniques in photography and review their submissions while giving them tips to improve.
  • Our vision for the photography club is to create a vibrant and inspiring community of passionate photographers.
